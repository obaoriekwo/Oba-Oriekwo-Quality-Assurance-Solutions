Meet the software Quality assurance Engineer

I am Oba Oriekwo, a software quality assurance engineer/full stack developer. I hold a bachalor's degree in Engineering and a master's degree in Industrial management. In addition, I have completed different software training programmes over the years.
My journey started as a technical personnel in a bioenergy company in Finland before pursuing a career in software Engineering.
I worked with a java based programme used alongside human machine interface(HMI) to monitor heavy duty heating plants remotely while working as a technical personnel. Monitoring a plant remotely with softaware and HMI was fascinating to me, and this inspired me to go into software Engineering.
The software experience gained from the technical field has motivated me a lot in the software world. I have been in the software Environment for the last 5 years handling projects ranging from manual testing, Automation testing, front-end and backend-software development. So far, I have a solid experience in numerous industry-leading software tools, frameworks with everlearning mindset.
